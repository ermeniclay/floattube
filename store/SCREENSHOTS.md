# Store Visual Assets — Guide (FloatTube)

The Chrome Web Store requires a few images. Sizes below are the current
requirements.

## Required

### 1) Store icon
- **128×128 PNG** — already in `icons/icon128.png`. (The store also derives
  smaller sizes from your packaged icons.)

### 2) Screenshots (at least 1, up to 5)
- Size: **1280×800** or **640×400 PNG/JPG** (1280×800 strongly recommended).
- No excessive text, no misleading claims, show the real UI.

**Suggested 5 screenshots (capture on a real YouTube video):**
1. **Pop-out hero** — the floating always-on-top player over a desktop/another
   app, with the red pop-out button visible in the YouTube control bar.
   (Caption idea: "Watch on top of everything")
2. **SEO panel open** — the SEO tab with the score ring + hints + tags.
   ("See SEO score, tags & hints instantly")
3. **Stats tab** — the stats grid (views, engagement, daily, age...).
   ("All the numbers that matter")
4. **Tools tab** — speed, A-B repeat, screenshot, thumbnail, export.
   ("Speed, A-B loop, screenshots & more")
5. **Closed/launcher state** — the small right-edge tab next to the video.
   ("Stays out of your way until you need it")

### How to capture clean 1280×800 shots
1. Resize the browser window, or capture full screen and crop to 1280×800.
2. Windows: `Win+Shift+S` to snip, paste into Paint, resize canvas to 1280×800,
   export PNG.
3. Keep the panel and pop-out button clearly visible; avoid copyrighted video
   content in the frame where possible (use your own video or a neutral one).

## Optional but recommended

### 3) Small promo tile
- **440×280 PNG/JPG** — a simple branded tile: dark background, the ▶ red icon,
  "FloatTube" wordmark, tagline "Pop-Out Player & SEO".

### 4) Marquee promo (only if featured)
- **1400×560 PNG/JPG** — usually not needed for launch.

## Tips
- First screenshot = your billboard. Make #1 the pop-out floating player; that's
  your differentiator.
- Add a short caption bar at the top of each screenshot (optional) for clarity.
- Keep a consistent dark theme across all shots to look premium.
