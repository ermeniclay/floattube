## Quick troubleshooting

- **Pop-out button missing?** Refresh the YouTube tab; it appears in the player
  control bar (red icon).
- **Panel not showing?** Look for the small tab on the right edge — click it to
  open. The panel is closed by default.
- **Stats are blank?** Make sure you're on a video page (`youtube.com/watch?...`).
- **Like count shows "—"?** It's read from the page and can vary with YouTube's
  UI/language; this is expected.

## FAQ

**Does FloatTube collect my data?** No. Everything is read on the page and stored
locally on your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking.

**Which browsers are supported?** Chrome, Edge, and Opera (Chromium). The
floating player uses Picture-in-Picture.
